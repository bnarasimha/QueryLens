How to Use Our Company Info Chatbot

Our intelligent chatbot interface makes it easy to find detailed information about companies and more. Here's how you can use it:

  1. Open the Chatbot: Look for the chat icon in the bottom right corner of the page.
  2. Start a Conversation: Click on the chat icon to open the chatbot interface.
  3. Ask Your Question: Type in your query about a company or any related information you're looking for.
  4. Receive Instant Answers: The chatbot will provide you with relevant information based on your query.
  5. Follow-up Questions: Feel free to ask follow-up questions to get more specific details.
